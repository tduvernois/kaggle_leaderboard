Overview

Internal kaggle competition to improve the scoring models used to predict a score to each Force alert. There are two datasets from two existing clients: Liberty US and Liberty Spain
To submit a result, you need to follow the below steps in the order:
  • Create team
  • Download datasets
  • Upload predictions
  • Access to leaderboard

The submitted files need to have a specific format as below:
    
      id;status
      1;0
      2;1
      3;1
      4;0
